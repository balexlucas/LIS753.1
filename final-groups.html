2026 FIFA World Cup - What you need to know

The Draw

On December 5th, 2025, the FIFA World Cup draw determined the composition of the groups at the 2026 World Cup. The groups are displayed below.

Note that there are six spots still open, the qualification process for which will conclude in March 2026. Each open spot represents one of six pathways, which are being competed for by three or four nations. For instance, EUR Pathway A will be taken by the winner of a playoff series between Bosnia & Herzegovina, Italy, Wales, and Northern Ireland.
